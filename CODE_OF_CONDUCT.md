# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful
- No harassment

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Warnings or bans may be applied depending on severity.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from Contributor Covenant.
